Site Name

AI in Video Games — This name directly reflects the central theme of the project: the presence and evolution of artificial intelligence in electronic games.

Site Purpose

To inform and inspire gamers and enthusiasts about how AI influences mechanics, characters, difficulty, and narrative in video games. The site is educational, with examples, analysis, and interactive resources.

Scenarios

  • Which games use AI to change the story based on player choices?
  • Which classic games marked creative use of AI?
  • How can AI adapt the difficulty to the player's style?
